About us


The "Yoga" is derived from the Sanskrit word "Yuj".The yoga definition comes from a very ancient and proven practice.It is originated from India and became very popular in the west during the last century.Physical exercises along with breathing techniques and meditation have been practiced since the beginning.It is a modern term that describe various levels of physical,mental and spiritual practices.

Yoga is way of living a purposeful and fulfilling life.It is a way of life even if there is not only one yoga definition,most agree that yoga means union,connection,harmony and balance.Moreover,yoga teaches a set of practices.Physical poses(asanas),breathing exercises(pranayama)and meditation(dhyana).Yoga is a powerful method to learn who you are.Yoga in itself is a very effective way to achieve freedom.By practicing yoga,we find liberation from unhealthy desires,emotions and thoughts that lead to suffering.It helps us shape our mind and the way we think.Yoga lets us discover our complete potential and achieve anything.

The Benefits of Yoga and Meditation.In today’s environment,many people are suffering with the stress and anxiety of day to day living and are finding themselves unable to cope with life’s little emergencies. Prescription drugs are on the rise due to the increasing need for people to deal with their stress and anxiety.However,many people are searching for ways to be less stressed and live a happy and healthy life without drugs.The benefits of yoga and meditation to create a more peaceful and healthy lifestyle.
